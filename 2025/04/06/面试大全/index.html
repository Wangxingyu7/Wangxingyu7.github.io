英文面试自我介绍

My name is Xingyu Wang, and I am an undergraduate student majoring in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics at Zhejiang University. I will graduate in July 2025.
During my studies, I have built a strong foundation in mathematics, including courses like Mathematical Analysis, Differential Geometry, Probability Theory, and Optimization Algorithms. My major GPA is in the top 29% of my class, and I have received awards such as the National Encouragement Scholarship and the Third-Class Scholarship from Zhejiang University.
I have also participated in several projects. For example, I worked on a semantic segmentation project where I used an encoder-decoder structure to improve image matting accuracy. Currently, I am working on my graduation thesis, focusing on robot path planning using curve interpolation. I designed algorithms to create smooth paths in images, using methods like Bezier quasi-average subdivision.

I am passionate about the intersection of mathematics and computational science and eager to join innovative research teams. Thank you!
