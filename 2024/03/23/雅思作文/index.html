ask one question at a time
can you assee my response and give me a score

12个词

skill ,techonlogy ,right ,environment,enployment,culture,

efficiency,safety,responsibility,health,money,fun

例题：Nowadays many people choose to be self-employed, rather than to work for a company or organisation. Why is this the case? What are the disadvantage of being self-employed?
(efficiency: it is more convenient to set up a company by ourselves)
(right: have full control over business decisions)
(fun: have more freedom and more flexible schedules)
缺点(right: no pension no paid vacations)
(right: have total responsibility for company)
(money: are not paid for overtime)
